The Problem

The Mechanics Trap

"AI tools let teams build software faster than ever.
But speed without direction just means arriving at the wrong destination faster."

Teams fall in love with what they can build — not with what users will come back for. The result: products that impress on first use and disappoint over time. More features. Deeper problems.

⚡

Built fast, abandoned faster

Vibe coding accelerates execution. It doesn't tell you what to build or why users will return.

🗺️

No shared map

Sales, onboarding, UX, and engineering each imagine a different journey. Nobody has a shared visual.

🔧

Tools optimize for building, not discovering

Every tool in the product stack is better at execution than at figuring out what to execute.

How It Works

From brief description
to team-ready storyboard

Three steps from idea to a vivid, shareable product journey.

STEP 01

Describe your product

Tell Journeymaker what you're building and who it's for. A sentence or two is enough to get started. No methodology required.

STEP 02

Journeymaker designs the journey

Our AI generates a plausible, opinionated journey — complete with wireframe-style storyboard panels showing each stage of the user experience.

STEP 03

Refine and share

Upload your pitch deck, customer interview transcripts, or team feedback to sharpen the design. Then share with your team, investors, or stakeholders.
