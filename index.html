How It Works

Can't draw?
Now you can prompt.

Crafting a storyboard takes time and skill most teams don't have — so they skip this crucial step entirely.

Just describe what your product does and who it's for. Journeymaker generates storyboards that show how customers discover, learn, and master your product. It fills in details based on leading products in your space — so you get an opinionated starting point, not a blank canvas.

The Methodology

Built on 25 years of
game-thinking research

Amy Jo Kim, Ph.D.
Co-Founder & CEO, Journeymaker

A strategist & trusted advisor.

Amy Jo Kim is a game designer and product strategist whose work has reached 100M+ people — including The Sims, Ultima Online, Rock Band, and Covet Fashion. She's worked alongside founders and product teams at Netflix, Disney, Google, Tesla, Replika, and Apollo Neuro, and created the Game Thinking methodology — a framework that distills the best practices of game design into a step-by-step system for building high-retention products.

She's a strategist and pattern-matcher who has seen what works — and what fails — across hundreds of products and dozens of categories. She challenges assumptions, names the blind spots, and sharpens your thinking.

After two decades of guiding product teams through journey design by hand, Amy Jo built Journeymaker to make that process fast and accessible — so every team can design the user journey before they build it, not after.
